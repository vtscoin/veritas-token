Veritas Token - Whitepaper

1. Introduction

Veritas Token (VTS) is a decentralized token created to promote transparency, truth, and trust in blockchain ecosystems.

2. Vision

Our vision is to build a platform that supports projects focused on education, transparency, and verifiable data.

3. Tokenomics

  • Total Supply: 1,000,000 VTS
  • Initial Liquidity: 30%
  • Team & Development: 20%
  • Marketing & Partnerships: 25%
  • Community & Airdrops: 25%

4. Utility

VTS will be used for governance, access to premium educational content, community rewards, and staking.

5. Roadmap

  • Q3 2025: Launch token and website
  • Q4 2025: Build community and form partnerships
  • Q1 2026: Platform beta release
